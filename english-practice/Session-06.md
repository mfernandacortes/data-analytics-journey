# Session 06 — Daily Standup Phrases
**Date:** 2026-06-12  
**Scenario:** Daily team standup meeting with tech lead Marcus  
**Duration:** ~10 minutes  

---

## Conversation summary

**Marcus:** Alright, quick standup. What are you working on today?

**Me:** I'm currently working on the sales dashboard.

**Marcus:** Great! When do you expect to have it done?

**Me:** I expect to finish this by Friday.

**Marcus:** Perfect. Any blockers I should know about?

**Me:** I don't have any blockers.

**Marcus:** Excellent! Keep up the good work.

---

## Key phrases learned today
1. **"I'm currently working on the [project]."** — For status updates
2. **"I expect to finish this by [date]."** — For timelines
3. **"I don't have any blockers."** — For signaling smooth progress

These three phrases are the foundation of every daily standup in remote teams.
