# Session 01 — Job Interview
**Date:** 2026-06-07  
**Scenario:** Remote Data Analyst position — interview with hiring manager (Sarah)  
**Duration:** ~15 minutes  

---

## Conversation summary

**Sarah:** Hi! Thanks for joining. Tell me a little about yourself — what's your background?

**Me:** Hi! I like it! I'm Data Analyst junior, I worked with a dataset about: Defensa del Consumidor. My skills: SQL Server: CTE, windows functions, a little pandas, Power BI. I worked with PHP, SQL.

**Sarah:** What kind of insights were you trying to find in the consumer defense data?

**Me:** In the project Defensa Consumidor: denuncias in pandemia... too I worked with dataset Northwind: find the best employee, products, quantity and money. I have a GitHub where I work every day, a repository data-analytics-journey. You can to see my portfolio: mfercortesdev.com.ar

**Sarah:** How do you manage your day when working from home?

**Me:** In my day I'm going to school, I'm teacher and I come back at three o'clock.

---

## Feedback received

### ✅ What went well
- Very understandable — the main goal!
- Technical vocabulary handled confidently (SQL, CTE, Power BI, dataset, repository)
- No freezing — responded with good flow
- Strong concrete projects to show (Defensa del Consumidor, Northwind)
- Daily GitHub habit is a great selling point

### 🔧 Things to polish
| What I said | Better version | Why |
|-------------|---------------|-----|
| "too" (at the end) | "also" / "additionally" | Sounds more professional |
| "you can to see" | "you can see" | No "to" after modal verbs |
| "denuncias" | "complaints" / "claims" | Translate domain-specific words |
| "I'm going to school" | "I work at a school" / "I teach at a school" | Avoid sounding like a student |
| "about: Defensa del Consumidor" | "related to consumer protection" | More natural preposition |

### 📝 New vocabulary learned
- **complaints / claims** = denuncias
- **consumer protection** = defensa del consumidor
- **async communication** = comunicación sin estar en tiempo real
- **deliverable** = entregable

---

## Overall assessment
**4/5** — Strong base for remote job hunting. Profile is attractive: teacher + data analyst with real projects and active GitHub. With daily 15-minute practice, expect a big confidence jump in 4-6 weeks.
