# Session 04 — Remote Work Tools
**Date:** 2026-06-09  
**Scenario:** First week at a new remote team — tech lead explains communication tools  

---

## Conversation

**Marcus:** Hey, welcome to the team! Let me give you a quick overview of how we work. We use Slack for daily communication, GitHub for code and documentation, and Notion for project tracking. Are you familiar with any of these?

**Me:** Hi! Thanks! I know GitHub for code, I've used Slack before, and I'm not familiar with Notion but I'm happy to learn it.

**Marcus:** Perfect! GitHub and Slack are the most important ones, so you're already set. Notion is pretty intuitive, I'll share some resources with you. How do you prefer to communicate — do you like async messages or quick calls when you have a question?

**Me:** I'd prefer async messages. What about team meetings?

**Marcus:** We have a daily standup every morning — it's just 10 minutes on Google Meet. And once a week we have a longer team meeting on Fridays. Does that work for you?

**Me:** Great, I'm available from 2pm.

**Marcus:** Perfect, that works well with our schedule. We usually do standups at 2pm anyway. Looking forward to working with you!
